Introduction to Origami

Origami is the art of paper folding, which is often associated with Japanese culture.
In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices, regardless of their culture of origin.

Fun Facts About Animals

How to make Camel

  • Camel are domestic animals.
  • Camel can live for 7 months without water.
  • Camel lives in hot locations.

How to make Panda.

  • Panda are domestic animals.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  • Pandas have so many fans because they look cute.

How to make chameleon.

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads.
  • Chameleons come in a lot of different shapes and sizes.
  • Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues.

How to make cicada.

  • Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans.
  • Cicadas inundate forests as a survival mechanism.
  • Cicadas are not locusts.
